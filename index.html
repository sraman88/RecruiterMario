Recruiter Run

Play as Recruiter Mario in an office. Shoot offers (S) to candidates. Some accept, some throw offers back — dodge them! Level up your role as you recruit more.

How to play

  • Move left/right — keyboard or on-screen (mobile). Jump to dodge.
  • Press S to shoot an offer (envelope). Candidates may accept or throw it back.
  • Recruit required number of candidates to level up; costumes change with role.
  • Good managers spawn occasionally to give a magnet (pulls candidates). Bad managers snatch candidates or shoot meeting invites.
  • Reach the flag to complete a level. Fireworks celebrate success!
